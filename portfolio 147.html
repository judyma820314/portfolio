Creating DITA-Ready Content with AI

My team spent hours manually converting Word documents into DITA-compliant XML for our global learning platform. I built a 5-step AI workflow using UAI Booster to auto-generate structured, topic-based DITA files and trained the team to adopt it. Result: 30% less time on content authoring.
