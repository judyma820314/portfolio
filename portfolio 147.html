Enterprise L&D AI Workflow

SAP · 2025

AI-Powered Video Editing Workflow

Designed a reusable 10-step AI workflow to transform raw video scripts into publish-ready learning videos — cutting video editing time by 50%. The workflow uses Microsoft Copilot to clean and restructure SRT subtitles, Azure AI to regenerate professional voiceovers, and Camtasia for final post-production.

50%
Video editing time saved
10
Step repeatable workflow

Workflow steps:

1. Upload to LSC → extract SRT 2. Strip timestamps via Copilot 3. Reformat for Azure AI voice 4. Remove redundant words 5. Logic & flow check 6. Add opening & closing 7. Generate audio in Azure AI 8. Combine audio & video in Camtasia 9. Add effects, animation & thumbnail 10. Export MP4 with embedded captions
Microsoft Copilot Azure AI Voice Camtasia Prompt Engineering LSC

Prompt-driven workflow designed to align AI-generated audio with existing video, minimising re-editing effort.
